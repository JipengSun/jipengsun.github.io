Push Human-Machine Co-Evolution

Humans empower machines, machines enhance humans.

Jipeng Sun regards pushing the co-evolution of humans and machines forward as his mission. He and his 136-people organization Sharing Tech Ltd. are working on enhancing humans with machines and exploring artificial general intelligence in a nature-intelligence way.

How to gain knowledge quickly and efficiently? To enhance the learning ability of humans, Jipeng Sun founded Sharing Ideas in 2016, trying to find the best knowledge representation in the age of the internet and build an open-sourced knowledge learning platform. On the AGI exploring side, Jipeng Sun joined the Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences (CASIA) as a brain-inspired intelligence researcher in 2019. His research focuses on the bodily self-consciousness model for robotics, trying to reproduce the body modeling process of human beings using spiking neural networks.

Currently, to learn how to build out AR glasses with the brain-machine interface, Jipeng Sun started his MSCS study at Northwestern University, learning computer graphics, computer vision, and embedded systems. He focuses his research on computer-generated holography and light field imaging. He is going to continue his research in this area at Computational Photography Lab as a Ph.D. student in Sept 2022. Co-advised by Prof. Olliver Cossairt, Prof. Jack Tumblin, Prof. Emma Alexander, and Prof. Florian Willomitzer.
