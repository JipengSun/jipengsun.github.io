Push Human-Machine Co-Evolution

Humans empower machines, machines enhance humans.

Jipeng Sun is currently conducting computational imaging research at Computational Photography Lab at Northwestern co-advised by Prof. Oliver Cossairt, Prof. Jack Tumblin, Prof. Emma Alexander, Prof. Florian Willomitzer, and Prof. Goeffrey Goodhill as a PhD student.

Jipeng is fascinated about building machines that enhance humans' vision, learning, and decision making systems and empower machines with biological inspired intelligence. To fulfill this goal, he has diverse background in computational microscopy, computer generated holography, knowledge graph, spiking neural networks, neural-robotics and embeded system.

Before joining Northwestern, he worked as neural-robotics research engineer at CASIA (Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences) during 2019-2021. He is also the founding director of Sharing Tech Ltd., a 136-people tech company developing AI-assisted education systems.
